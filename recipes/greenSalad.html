Green Salad

Direction

Description

Cook: 5 mins Total: 20 mins Prep: 15 mins Servings: 8 Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sliced almonds
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup fresh cranberries
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Arrange almonds in a single layer on a baking sheet. Toast in oven for 5 minutes, or until nuts begin to brown.
  2. Step 2 Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
